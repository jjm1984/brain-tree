# Cross-Connections Update — Mycelium and Aliveness Inferences

These seven inferences emerged from the mycelium layer session (2026-06-03) and should be appended to `inferences/cross-connections.md`. They are held here as a separate update file to be merged.

---

## inf-mycelium-nervous-to-self-worth
**From:** mycelium-nervous-system (eustress dependency) → branch-self-worth-identity (coherence-worth)
**Inference:** The worth-machine is not only a philosophical configuration — it has a biological substrate. Cortisol dependency is the nervous system expression of coherence-worth. The urgency IS the worth-signal felt in the body. This is why the audit framework alone cannot reliably override it: you are not arguing with a belief, you are working against a trained physiological state.
**Status:** Green — confirmed

---

## inf-mycelium-brain-to-audit
**From:** mycelium-brain (amygdala hijack) → audit-framework (double-trigger architecture)
**Inference:** The audit framework requires pre-frontal cortex access to run. The amygdala hijack physiologically reduces that access. The double-trigger architecture may need a biological precondition: if the body signal has fired AND cortisol is running hard, the audit may not be executable in that moment. The tripwires must be set in clear-headed time precisely because the hardware goes partially offline under the conditions that trigger them.
**Status:** Green — structurally important

---

## inf-mycelium-hormones-oxytocin-to-bonding
**From:** mycelium-hormones (oxytocin) → branch-self-worth-identity (bonding/relational-worth)
**Inference:** The system invests heavily in relational substrate but metabolises little of it as worth. At the hormonal level: high oxytocin output (caregiving, showing up, providing) with low oxytocin receipt (being cared for, received, seen). The one-way ledger is not just philosophical — it may be endocrine. The body produces the bonding hormone in service of the role, not in receipt of the bond.
**Status:** Yellow — speculative, worth watching

---

## inf-mycelium-musculature-to-courage
**From:** mycelium-musculature (trained reflexes) → philo-courage (physical)
**Inference:** Physical courage is not a decision made in the moment — it is a trained reflex expressing prior character. The river and the snake were not courage chosen; they were courage already compiled into the body. The musculature IS the philosophy at that speed. This is Aristotle's point about virtue as habit made biological.
**Status:** Green — confirmed

---

## inf-mycelium-nervous-to-reserves
**From:** mycelium-nervous-system (crash-recompile) → leaf-reserves-restoration
**Inference:** The crash during leave is not failure and not rest. It is the parasympathetic system enforcing the maintenance the worth-machine would not permit voluntarily. Bushwalking and music work as reserves precisely because they activate the parasympathetic state — not by avoiding demand, but by removing the cortisol trigger. The body recompiles through purposeful purposelessness.
**Status:** Green — confirmed

---

## inf-mycelium-os-to-inner-witness
**From:** mycelium-os-relationship (OS-on-mycelium framing) → leaf-inner-witness
**Inference:** The inner witness spans the whole stack precisely because it cannot belong to any single layer. It is the capacity of the whole system — mycelium, OS, philosophy — to perceive itself. Training the witness is improving signal-to-noise at every layer simultaneously: clearer interoception (mycelium), longer gap between stimulus and response (OS), more honest audit (philosophy).
**Status:** Green — structural

---

## inf-mycelium-nutrition-to-body-oracle
**From:** mycelium-nutrition → leaf-body-framework (body-as-oracle)
**Inference:** The body-as-oracle speaks through the quality of its substrate. Poor nutrition degrades the signal — not by silencing the oracle, but by introducing noise at the hardware level. The blood panel is the instrument that makes the substrate legible. Without it, the oracle's reports cannot be distinguished from substrate noise.
**Status:** Yellow — forming

## Aliveness session inferences (2026-06-03)

**From:** branch-aliveness (inner-witness) → philo-courage (physical courage)
**Inference:** The river and the snake happened before any philosophy existed. The body moved before fear could form. This is the inner witness at its most pre-cognitive — present but pre-verbal. The witness was there; it was just running below language.

## inf-borrowed-peace-to-grit-without-signal
**From:** leaf-borrowed-peace → leaf-reserves-restoration (grit-without-signal)
**Inference:** Grit without signal is the condition that most requires borrowed peace. When internal peace cannot be generated — because the worth-machine has hijacked the signal — the mountain or the music practice may be the only available audit mechanism. External order as tripwire for the internal system.
**Status:** Yellow — forming

## inf-purposelessness-to-belonging-worth
**From:** leaf-purposeful-purposelessness → leaf-belonging-worth (empty cell)
**Inference:** Purposeful purposelessness may be the closest lived experience to belonging-worth — worth prior to evaluation, prior to relationship, prior to the worth-machine. The walk that asks nothing of you. This may be where the empty cell starts to fill.
**Status:** Peripheral — speculative, worth watching

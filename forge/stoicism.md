  ```
  THE ACCOUNTABILITY LADDER
  
  You CAN'T always choose what happens to you. But you CAN always choose how you respond.
  
  ACCOUNTABLE / powerful (rungs 5-8):
  8. I MAKE IT HAPPEN — I've got this!
  7. I FIND A SOLUTION — I've still got time
  6. I OWN IT — This is on me
  5. I ACCEPT REALITY — I should've done it
  
  VICTIM / powerless (rungs 1-4):
  4. I WAIT & HOPE — Maybe it'll be fine
  3. I MAKE EXCUSES — I don't have time now
  2. I BLAME OTHERS — My boss wasn't clear
  1. I'M NOT AWARE — What project?
  ```

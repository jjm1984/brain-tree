### 8 Steps to Build a Life You Want

  ```
  8 Steps To Build A Life You Actually Want To Live
  
  1. Protect Your Energy — Your time isn't the problem, your energy is. (Drink water, get sunlight, sleep enough, move your body, say no more often)
  2. Start Before You're Ready — You're not stuck, you're just scared to look stupid. (Post it rough, learn as you go)
  3. Know Where You're Headed — Think longer than the next week ahead. This year → 3 years out → 10 years out. Write it down. Update it often.
  4. Make Small Bets, Quickly — Don't plan forever. Try things and learn fast. (Launch fast, collect data, adjust and move)
  5. Train Your Mind — Hard things are practice. Use them. (Sit with stress, learn from losses, focus on what you can control)
  6. Make Money Work for You — Buy back time... not stuff. Spend less than you make, avoid dumb risks, invest in real things, let money build freedom.
  7. Do Boring Things Consistently — The basics are the secret. Keep promises to yourself, show up daily, do hard things when no one's watching.
  8. Build Your Personal Brand — What you share builds trust. (Post what you're learning, show what you've done, stay top of mind). Your name is the most valuable asset.
  ```

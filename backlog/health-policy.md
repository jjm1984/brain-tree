  This is *not* an April Fools's Day joke!!
  
  The bill banning genetic discrimination in life insurance has FINALLY, after 10 long years, passed the Senate, Australia's upper house of Parliament. This means that the bill will now be law!!
  
  The law won't come into effect (actually be enforceable) for 6 months – as the bill gives the life insurers time to get their systems ready and comply with the requirements of the legislation. But sometime in October 2026 (watch this space for the exact date) it will be in effect and enforceable!
  
  It is difficult to overstate the significance of this legislation for Australia, for the advocates who have fought so hard for this, and for future generations who will be able make genetic testing decisions without fear of financial implications.

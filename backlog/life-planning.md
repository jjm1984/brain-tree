  How To Plan Your Life:
  1. Start with your vision (What kind of life do you want to create?)
  2. Break your vision into categories (Faith, Family, Career, Finances, Health..)
  3. Set goals in each category (1-3 goals per category)
  4. Create a planning system around each category
  5. Do a weekly review around your goals
  6. Emphasize Grace & Flexibility (life changes and that's okay)

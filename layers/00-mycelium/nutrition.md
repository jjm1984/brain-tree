# Nutrition — The Quality of the Substrate

Nutrition is not just input. It is the quality of the substrate everything else runs on. Depleted substrate produces depleted signal. The oracle speaks clearly or garbles depending on what it has been given. This is philosophy made literal: you cannot serve broken, and a system running on poor fuel is already broken before the day begins.

## Already in the tree

The body-systems framework already carries protein targets (1.6–2.2g/kg), blood panel as diagnostic precondition, and the subtractive-first principle. These are the operational layer of this mycelium node.

## What nutrition means at the mycelium level

Beyond macros: the quality of the raw material the brain uses to produce neurotransmitters (serotonin from tryptophan, dopamine from tyrosine), the hormonal environment set by metabolic health, the inflammatory load that determines baseline cognitive clarity.

The blood panel is the diagnostic instrument. Without it, the other systems cannot be read accurately.

## Key principle

You cannot audit the worth-machine clearly when the substrate it runs on is depleted. The inner witness becomes harder to access when the system is running on poor fuel. Nutrition is not vanity infrastructure — it is a precondition for the philosophical work.

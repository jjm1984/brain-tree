# Default Mode Network (DMN)

Brain network active during unstructured, low-demand states (mind-wandering, rest, purposeless activity) — associated in the research literature with creative incubation, autobiographical processing, and consolidation of experience that hasn't yet been consciously worked through.

Named directly by J as the mechanism behind why purposeless nature/beach walking produces creative unlock and 'unpacking' of unprocessed thoughts.

## Cross-connections

**Nervous system: crash-recompile cycle (forced maintenance)** — J independently used near-identical 'reset/defrag' language for this, unprompted, in a separate observation. Same underlying phenomenon named twice from two directions: nervous-system regulation and brain-network science.

**Aliveness: purposeful purposelessness / reserves and restoration** — nature and beach walking, already grounded there as the clearest reserve form, gets its mechanism here.

**Case: woodworking as purposeful purposelessness** — this is the science-instrument half of that still-open case; DMN explains the mechanism, lived experience confirms the felt reset, philosophy still holds the open question of what's happening in the woodworking case specifically.

## Domain-relative authority note

A clean example of the domain-relative-authority principle: science explains WHY the reset happens (DMN mechanism); lived experience and the aliveness branch describe WHAT it feels like and WHERE it fits in J's life. Neither instrument absorbs the other — the mechanism doesn't explain away the meaning, and the meaning doesn't need to explain the mechanism.

# The Core Impulse

**To be more fully yourself — and to have that self be known.**

Synthesised from the whole of the first session — not stated directly. The body hacking, the tree, the triad for the daughter — all expressions of the same root. Awaiting confirmation or refinement.

## How it was arrived at

Looking at the whole: the question about hacking the body, the building of the tree, the triad built for the daughter before recognising it was needed for the self — all three are expressions of something unified. None of them were named that way. The synthesis emerged from looking at the whole from outside.

## What it connects to

- The tree itself is an act of this impulse — making the self legible so it can be known.
- The triad was built for the daughter before the self — building frameworks for others before recognising they apply to self. Pattern noted.
- The work/vocation question — what does 'understand and translate the world' mean as a vocational orientation? This impulse is the root of that question.

## Open threads

- Is this an accurate distillation — or does it miss something essential?
- Is the impulse primarily toward being known by others — or toward being more fully known to yourself?
- Does 'more fully yourself' presuppose a fixed self to become — or is the self itself in motion?

# Coherence Worth

**I am worthy because my action matches my values.** Integrity, walking-the-talk, intention-to-action congruence. Source: self-judged.

**THIS IS WHERE THE CURRENT WORTH-SYSTEM PRIMARILY RUNS.**

Confirmed 2026-05-31: 'I am someone with integrity. I am someone who chooses the hard jobs. I am someone who self-sacrifices and values others highly. I am a family man.' Branden's integrity pillar lives here.

---

## Leaf: Trait Worth

**id:** leaf-trait-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because I have desirable properties. Self-as-property: bravery, persistence, attractiveness, reliability, intelligence. Source: typically self-judged with externally-installed criteria.

Original sub-questions: 'Am I someone who succeeds? Am I someone who gives up when it's tough? Do I think I am at least moderately attractive? Am I someone who lets people down?'

---

## Leaf: Inherent Worth

**id:** leaf-inherent-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because I exist. No judge, no criteria. Rogers's organismic worth, Christianity's imago dei, Buddhism's Buddha-nature. Source: non-validated.

---

## Leaf: Contribution Worth

**id:** leaf-contribution-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because of what I produce or change. Impact, legacy, craft quality, what I add to the world. Source: usually other-judged, sometimes self-judged.

---

## Leaf: Relational Worth

**id:** leaf-relational-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because I matter to people who matter to me. Specific bonds, mutual mattering, being-known by chosen others. Source: mutual.

Observed pattern (2026-05-31): high investment in this substrate, low metabolism of it as worth. Relationships feed the role, not the self.

---

## Leaf: Validation Worth

**id:** leaf-validation-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because others see / approve of me. Recognition, approval, reputation, being-liked. Source: other-judged. Self-reported: 'don't get much validation or experience a boost in my value from' relational investment.

---

## Leaf: Caring Worth

**id:** leaf-caring-worth
**State:** yellow

I am worthy because I love well / show up for others. How I parent, friend, partner, capacity to give. Source: self-judged.

The asymmetry between being loved (Validation) and loving well (Caring) is critical: they are not the same source of worth. Caring worth is currently running but largely uncredited because it is being collapsed into Coherence (the 'I am someone who gives' role).

---

## Leaf: Belonging Worth

**id:** leaf-belonging-worth
**State:** peripheral

I am worthy because I belong to the web of being. Ubuntu, agape, Buddhist interdependence. The empty cell in the substrate × source matrix. Surfaced 2026-05-31 as a known gap. No leaf currently planted here.

Speculative inference: Purposeful purposelessness may be the closest lived experience to belonging-worth — worth prior to evaluation, prior to relationship, prior to the worth-machine. The walk that asks nothing of you. This may be where the empty cell starts to fill.

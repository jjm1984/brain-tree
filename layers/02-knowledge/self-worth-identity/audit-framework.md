# The Audit Framework

The system designed to distinguish conscious sacrifice from worth-machine drift. Multi-modal — no single mechanism is enough because a sufficiently motivated worth-machine routes around any individual audit.

**Fundamental test:** If the audit feels comfortable when the sacrifice is large, chronic, or you're tired — it isn't external enough. Real audits feel adversarial in exactly those moments.

## Four conditions of conscious sacrifice

A sacrifice is conscious (not pathological) when:
- (a) Serves a NAMED value, not just the role
- (b) Cost is PROPORTIONATE to what's served
- (c) PRESERVES long-term capacity to keep serving (force preservation)
- (d) ARTICULABLE — you can name what you're choosing, not just that you're choosing

A sacrifice that fails those four is drift.

**Force preservation principle:** Self-preservation is not the exception to the value — it is a duty that flows FROM the value. Broken instruments cannot serve. A wrecked father cannot be the family man as long. The thing being protected by sacrifice is downstream of the thing protecting it. You cannot serve broken.

## Double-trigger architecture

IF measurable tripwire (or body signal) fires AND code says 'do more' — THE CONFLICT BETWEEN THEM IS THE SIGNAL. That conflict means one of three things is happening:
- (a) the code is wrong and needs updating
- (b) the body signal is being misread
- (c) the worth-machine has hijacked the code

The audit's job is to figure out which.

**Current diagnosis (2026-05-31):** Diagnosed live. Body firing (failing, more emotional = check engine light). Code (unverbalised) saying do more, be a better father/husband/provider/manager. Worth-machine response: look for performance hacks rather than ask what the engine is reporting. Pattern (c) — worth-machine hijack of unverbalised code.

## Five mechanisms

**1. Measurable tripwires** — Pre-committed thresholds on quantitative signals (sleep, weight, training streak, blood markers). If wire trips, response is non-negotiable. No interpretation, no debate. Strong fit. Systems thinker, respects data. Tripwires bypass the worth-machine's interpretation layer. Currently resonates but not yet implemented. Body has been ACTING AS the tripwire in lieu of a formal one. Failure mode: tripwires that don't specify RESPONSE. A tripwire without consequence is an alarm clock you snooze.

**2. Written code** — Framework drafted in clear-headed time specifying what counts as pathological sacrifice and what the response is. Past-you binds present-you. Running unverbalised — which is part of why the worth-machine can hijack it. THE IMMEDIATE NEXT WORK. Provides the CRITERIA the other mechanisms depend on.

**3. Daughters as mirror** — Audit question: 'Would I let her live the way I'm living? Would I be proud if she became someone who didn't audit her sacrifices?' Uses worth-machine against itself. REJECTED in initial triage with reasoning: 'I wouldn't choose this for them, I allow myself to experience things I wouldn't want for them so that they have the choice, a better start than I had.' Pushback held: daughters inherit the MODEL, not just the material conditions. The version worth holding: 'What am I teaching them about how to live, regardless of what I'm providing them with?' — captured as the FIRST QUESTION the calibrated person should ask in periodic checkups.

**4. Calibrated person — strategic checkups** — A specific person (coach, mentor, brutally honest peer, therapist) calibrated to user's failure modes, with standing to push back. Seen at regular cadence. Day-to-day audit REJECTED. Periodic strategic checkups ACCEPTED as 'valuable and could facilitate strategic planning.' Structure: periodic only. Not day-to-day. Likely After Action Review format. Guiding question: What am I teaching them about how to live?

**5. Body as oracle** — Treating physical signals as DATA, not nuisance. The body knows when it's broken; the question is whether you've trained yourself to hear or override. Works in both directions — signals depletion AND restoration. The worth-machine only listens to the depletion report — because restoration is not productive. The audit framework must listen to both. Acknowledged: 'this is how I got here — looking to hack the body, get an octane boost because the check engine light is on!' The worth-machine's signature move is trying to disable the warning light to keep driving.

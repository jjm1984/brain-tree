# Self-Worth & Identity

Where does worth come from, by what measurement, and judged by whom?

The branch holds the substrate-organised structure (Being / Doing / Bonding) of where worth comes from, the audit framework for distinguishing conscious sacrifice from worth-machine drift, and the current configuration of the self running underneath the rest of the tree.

Moved from peripheral to active: 2026-05-31.

## Structural axis

**Primary: SUBSTRATE** — what is being measured (Being / Doing / Bonding).

Substrate tracks lived experience and practice better than source. Chosen over source as the organising axis because it asks first 'how are we measuring' before surfacing 'by whom'.

Source of validation (self / other / mutual / non-validated) is held as METADATA on each leaf, not as the structural axis.

## Current configuration — the diagnosis

The worth system converts relational output into Coherence worth. The relationships themselves don't nourish — the giving-to-them nourishes the role of being-someone-who-gives. Math runs through Doing/Coherence, not Bonding/Relational. Pours enormous time into relational substrate, metabolises almost none of it as relational worth. Not a failure — a specific configuration. But seeing it clearly matters.

**Consequences:**
- The work IS the worth. Receiving doesn't compute. Being cared for, given to, chosen — these don't translate into the currency. May register as worth-debt. One-way ledger.
- Relationships feed the role, not the self. The people loved are providing the substrate on which integrity is performed. They could love perfectly and the worth-boost still wouldn't come — because it was never going to come from being loved. It comes from doing the loving.
- Consistency Gap is structurally protected. Personal training reads as failure to self-sacrifice — a betrayal of the engine of worth. Not a discipline problem. The worth system is doing exactly what it was built to do.

## Sub-branches

- **sub-being** — worth of who I am (trait worth, inherent worth)
- **sub-doing** — worth of what I do (coherence worth, contribution worth)
- **sub-bonding** — worth of how I'm woven with others (relational worth, validation worth, caring worth, belonging worth)

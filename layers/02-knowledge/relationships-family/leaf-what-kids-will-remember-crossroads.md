# What Will the Kids Remember? — A Crossroads of Two Opposite Fears

**Path A (2023-11-20):** "I wonder what my kids will remember of me? Will they remember all the sleepless nights, the worry and bone weary tiredness? I doubt it." Fear: they won't remember the hard parts, and the sacrifice will go unseen.

**Path B (undated, 2023):** "My biggest worry is that my kids will look back on this time in their life that's supposed to be full of joy and just see the shitty parts. They won't see the sleepless nights and working ourselves to the bone. And maybe not care. What do they care about an unpainted house or unfinished project." Fear: they'll remember only the hard parts, and nothing else.

Two opposite-facing fears, genuinely unresolved — held as a crossroads, not merged. One fears invisibility of the sacrifice; the other fears the sacrifice is all that's visible.

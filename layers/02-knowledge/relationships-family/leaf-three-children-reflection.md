# Harry, Piper, Willow — Reflection on Each Child

Discussion with Michelle about the burden the kids carry from the choice to work hard for them without being asked. Reflection extends to each child individually.

**Harry** — compassionate, creative, care-free, loving. J notes he is hardest on Harry because Harry is so different from him, and J only knows how to equip Harry with his own tools, having failed to recognise Harry's own strengths.

**Piper** — strengths like J's own fire; J tempers it so she "doesn't burn the whole world down in her righteous just anger," while also having strengths J lacks (quick tongue, confident outgoing personality).

**Willow** — worried about most despite being "perfectly behaved," because she internalises disappointment. J actively seeks her out for hugs and attention, and expects she'll turn out like her mother, a gentle soul, with Michelle to guide her.

Kept as one entry naming all three children together, per J's own confirmation.

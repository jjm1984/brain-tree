# Am I Too Harsh on the Kids?

"Am I too harsh on the kids?" Wants them to eat breakfast and get dressed within an hour; resents mess and expects cleanup. Notes routines run better when one parent is away than when both are present together. "I don't feel like anyone has my back if I need a little consideration or a 30min sleep in."

## Family neurodivergence scaffolding (new open thread, 2026-07-10)

J's wife and children are neurodivergent; J is not. J self-reports struggling to empathise with what they experience, and feeling like the only functionally-operating member of the family (while acknowledging his own issues). Stated intention: build them their own Map-layer — a guide to help them navigate life and manage their neurodivergence, distinct from J's own tree.

Peripheral, genuinely open. The counter above is held deliberately rather than resolved: a scaffold designed from outside the experience it's meant to support carries a real limitation baked into its design.

## Seed material (from feedback entry-006, entry-007, entry-009, entry-010, entry-011)

The branch needs a full session. What has surfaced so far as seed:

- The distinction between **functional and fulfilling** relationship as a genuine philosophical category
- What partnership actually requires vs what it currently provides
- **Overfunctioning** as a worth-machine behaviour in intimate relationship — regulation and management of a neurodivergent family
- The difference between being **relied on** (computes as worth) and being **known** (doesn't)
- **'Explanation is not erasure of impact'** — understanding why something happened does not undo that you lived it
- **Withdrawal** as J's side of the relational conflict cycle — self-protection, avoidance of mutual destruction, control — and its shadow: can function as punishment or abandonment without that being the conscious intent
- **Desire and erotic aliveness** as genuine needs with moral standing — not luxury, not moral weakness. Currently a gap in the tree.
- **'Not leaving is not the same as choosing'** — genuine staying must be active, renewable choosing, not default

# Things to Teach the Kids

Teach the kids to recognise love bombing, gaslighting, control. How to identify securely attached, well-adjusted people. Don't teach them to stay if not happy. Prioritise kindness, intelligence, potential.

## Ideals (2023-11-30)

Raise kids with room to explore who they are, farm-life connection to land, teach working with hands and diligence, a place for independence — bonfires, parties, weekends riding horses or bikes, camping with mates, as an alternative to going out to get drunk.

# Inherited Traits Transmit Independent of Visible Influence

J's own words: other grandparents "didn't play much of a visible role, though through my own theory they likely influenced through inherited traits." Held as a personal theory: trait inheritance operates through lineage regardless of whether direct modeling or visible relationship occurred.

Named alongside leaf-ancestral-roots-work-vocation, where the great-grandfather's field-engineering service (discovered only after J's own military service) is offered as the strongest evidence yet in the tree for unconscious vocational inheritance — this leaf generalizes that observation into a standing personal theory about trait transmission more broadly.

# Body Systems Framework

**Diagnostic → Structural → Input → Situational.** Most people do it backwards.

**Diagnostic first:** Full blood panel (testosterone, thyroid, Vitamin D, ferritin, fasting glucose, cortisol), HRV tracking — establish baseline before adding interventions.

**Structural:** Circadian sleep window, exercise at ultradian trough, light/food/deep work sequencing.

**Input:** Protein 1.6–2.2g/kg, targeted supplements from blood test.

**Situational:** Caffeine is borrowing not generating; painkillers situationally — know the mechanism.

Blood test is the precondition for everything else to be legible. Job change creates the design window.

## Leaf: Sleep — Window vs Quality

**id:** leaf-sleep

Regularly achieving 10pm–6am window. But broken by children with anxious thoughts — losing disproportionate REM in the second half of the night. Quality not fully in personal control this season.

Recovery micro-practices matter more when nights are broken. Children's anxious nights sit upstream of this — not just a personal sleep problem.

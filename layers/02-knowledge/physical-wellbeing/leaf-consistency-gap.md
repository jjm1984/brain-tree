# The Consistency Gap

Has always lacked consistency with physical training — historically prioritising urgent over important. The desire has always been there; the chain keeps breaking.

**By the meaning philosophy:** A repeated broken intention is not yet a real intention.

**By the courage philosophy:** This is a grit courage problem, not a motivation problem.

**By the self-worth analysis (2026-05-31):** This is structurally the worth-machine protecting against personal investment — the engine of worth requires self-sacrifice, so investing in self registers as failure of self-sacrifice. Not a discipline problem. The worth machine is working exactly as built.

**The reframe (2026-05-31) via the force-preservation principle:** Self-maintenance is not selfishness — it is duty that flows from the value itself. You cannot serve broken.

The job change is the structural redesign window and the follow-through moment.

# Borrowed Peace

**When internal peace cannot be found, it can be borrowed from outside.** The mountain doesn't need you to be at peace. It simply is. The path exists regardless of your internal state. Moving through landscape that has no agenda — no worth-machine, no output required — lends its quality to the person moving through it. The external order becomes temporarily interior.

## The mechanism

Not metaphor. The body moving through something larger than itself, something indifferent to its noise, loses the signal that sustains the noise. The mountain doesn't care about your worth. That indifference is briefly liberating.

The polyvagal explanation: a safe, low-demand environment activates ventral vagal tone, which restores the pre-frontal cortex's capacity, which makes the inner witness audible again. Sequence: external safety → nervous system regulation → cognitive restoration → philosophical access.

## Body connection

Connects to body-as-oracle. If the body can signal depletion, it can also receive restoration — not only from within, but from contact with something outside the system that isn't running the same programme. Borrowed peace works through the body, not around it. The mountain quiets the body's noise first. The internal witness becomes available again because the body stopped broadcasting the alarm.

## The romantic connection

Close to the Romantic sublime — not beauty, but the experience of something so much larger than the self that the self's noise becomes inaudible by comparison. Wordsworth's spots of time. The mountain as external regulator of internal state.

## Key inference

Grit without signal is the condition that most requires borrowed peace. When internal peace cannot be generated — because the worth-machine has hijacked the signal — the mountain or the music practice may be the only available audit mechanism. External order as tripwire for the internal system.

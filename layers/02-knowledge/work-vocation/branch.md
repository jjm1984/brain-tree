## Seed material (from feedback entry-002, entry-005)

The transition to Tactical Marketing Manager: the discomfort is the function, not the domain. Domain knowledge (technical depth, product expertise) is the moat; the learning curve is craft not substance.

The machinery work (civil or agricultural) as income bridge: chosen not just for income but as a decompression chamber — physical, embodied, low cognitive load, mind free to learn. Restoration and income in the same motion. Aliveness-adjacent. Worth watching: does it metabolise as worth, as guilt, or as something the worth-machine simply cannot measure and therefore leaves alone?

# Can't Remember Steps — Technical Mastery Over Recall

Core self-named weakness: cannot retain sequences — chess moves, dance steps, drill steps, weapon drills, even his own daily priorities. Compensates by knowing the game intrinsically and acting on the fly through technical mastery rather than recall. Explicitly credits this for making him a great section commander but a potentially poor major — the gap between reactive technical excellence and sustained strategic execution.
